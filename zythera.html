About the Project

Co-Developed with Snckish

Zythera was built in collaboration with Snckish as a full-stack furniture e-commerce platform, combining a polished customer-facing storefront with a complete admin side for running the business.

Shopping Cart & Checkout

Customers can browse the catalog, add pieces to their cart, and move through a complete checkout flow — the full shopping experience you'd expect from a real online furniture store.

Inventory & Order Tracking

An admin dashboard handles inventory tracking and order management, keeping product stock and incoming orders in sync as the store operates.

Responsive Across Devices

Built with PHP, Bootstrap, and MySQL, the interface was designed to stay polished and usable whether customers are browsing on desktop or mobile.

Key Features

Product Catalog

Furniture pieces are organized into a clean, browsable catalog so customers can find what they're looking for without friction.

Cart & Checkout Flow

A guided shopping cart and checkout process takes customers from browsing to completed order with minimal steps.

Admin Dashboard

Store administrators get a dedicated panel for managing products, monitoring orders, and updating inventory in real time.

Order Management

Incoming orders are tracked from placement through fulfillment, giving the team a clear view of what needs attention.
